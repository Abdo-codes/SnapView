# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want participation in the `snapview` project to be respectful, constructive, and free of harassment.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, but not hostile.
- Focus on the code, behavior, and product decisions.
- Assume good intent while still being technically rigorous.
- Accept feedback without personal attacks.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation
- Personal insults or bad-faith engagement
- Publishing private information without consent
- Disruptive conduct that makes collaboration unsafe or unproductive

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or ban participants who violate this code of conduct.

For reporting, use the private security/contact path described in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
